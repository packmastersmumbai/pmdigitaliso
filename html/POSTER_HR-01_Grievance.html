Process Flow  /  प्रक्रिया प्रवाह
1
Verbal Grievance to Supervisor
Raise your concern verbally with your immediate Supervisor or Line Manager.
Within 3 Working Days
2
Written Submission to HR
If unresolved, submit a written grievance to the HR Department using the Grievance Form.
If Unresolved at Step 1
3
Committee Review
The Grievance Committee examines the written complaint and gathers information from all parties.
Within 7 Working Days
4
Written Decision Communicated
The Committee communicates its findings and decision in writing to the employee.
After Committee Review
5
Appeal to Management Rep.
If dissatisfied with the Committee decision, appeal to the Management Representative.
Within 3 Days of Decision
6
Final Appeal — Top Management
Final appeal to Top Management. The decision of Top Management is binding and conclusive.
Decision is Final & Binding
