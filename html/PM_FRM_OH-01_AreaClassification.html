General Safety Rules / सामान्य सुरक्षा नियम

  1. No smoking, open flames, or spark-producing equipment in RED and YELLOW zones.
  2. Mandatory PPE must be worn before entering any classified area as indicated above.
  3. SDS (Safety Data Sheets) must be available at each workstation handling chemicals.
  4. Spill kits and fire extinguishers must be checked monthly and kept unobstructed at all times.
