DANGER खतरा
CR & PU Based Adhesives Handling Area CR और PU आधारित एडहेसिव हैंडलिंग एरिया
Hazards — खतरे
Flammable Solvents in CR adhesive are highly flammable. Keep away from all ignition sources. ज्वलनशील — CR एडहेसिव में सॉल्वेंट अत्यधिक ज्वलनशील हैं। इग्निशन स्रोतों से दूर रखें।
Health Hazard PU adhesive contains isocyanates. Causes respiratory sensitization. Vapours are harmful if inhaled. स्वास्थ्य खतरा — PU एडहेसिव में आइसोसायनेट हैं। सांस की समस्या हो सकती है। वाष्प हानिकारक है।
Irritant Skin and eye contact causes irritation and chemical burns. Avoid all direct contact. त्वचा और आंखों में जलन तथा केमिकल बर्न्स का खतरा। सीधे संपर्क से बचें।
Mandatory Rules — अनिवार्य नियम
1
VENTILATION — Ensure exhaust ventilation is ON before opening any container.
2
PPE — Wear chemical gloves, safety goggles, and chemical apron at ALL times.
3
NO FLAMES — No smoking, no open flames, no sparks within 5 metres.
4
SEQUENCE — Add materials in specified sequence only. Do NOT deviate from product spec.
5
SPILLS — Contain immediately. Use spill kit. Alert Supervisor without delay.
6
WASH — Wash hands and all exposed skin thoroughly after handling.

First Aid — प्राथमिक उपचार
Eyes — आंखें Flush with clean water for 15 minutes. Seek medical attention immediately.
Skin — त्वचा Remove contaminated clothing. Wash affected area with soap and water thoroughly.
Inhalation — सांस Move to fresh air immediately. If breathing difficulty persists, seek medical help.
Ingestion — निगलना Do NOT induce vomiting. Rinse mouth. Call doctor / poison control immediately.
